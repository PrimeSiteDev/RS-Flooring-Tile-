The Transformation

Before & After

This master bathroom was completely reimagined from the ground up. The original space featured a dated jacuzzi tub, a cramped shower stall with a basic glass door, plain beige walls, and worn tile flooring. Our team gutted the entire room and delivered a stunning, modern retreat — complete with an elegant freestanding soaking tub, a spacious open walk-in shower with matte black fixtures, and floor-to-ceiling herringbone tile work that ties the whole space together.

After

The New Space

A freestanding soaking tub sits on a raised platform of herringbone mosaic tile, flanked by floor-to-ceiling wood-look porcelain and a custom accent wall. Matte black fixtures, a built-in niche, and recessed lighting complete the spa-like atmosphere.

Before

The Original Bathroom

A dated jacuzzi tub with basic white tile surround sat next to a cramped shower stall with a framed glass door. Plain beige walls, worn tile flooring, and outdated fixtures left the space feeling tired and uninspired.
